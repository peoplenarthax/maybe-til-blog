This week, I participated in a series of workshop program provided by [Datadog](https://www.datadoghq.com/) for managers. To be honest, I've always viewed management as common sense (mainly). Consequently, I tend to approach books, lessons, and talks with a bit of skepticism. This week served as a reminder that these resources are just there to provide structure and "names" to concepts we most likely understand as well as offering some food for thought.

The focus of the lesson was on feedback/coaching, and specifically on the concept of trust. I have never seen [Charles H. Green's](https://trustedadvisor.com/consultants/charles-h-green) definition:

> **Trustworthiness = (Credibility + Reliability + Intimacy) / Self-orientation**
